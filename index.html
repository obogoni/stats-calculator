Pokémon Stats Calculator

Formulas Used:

HP: HP = ⌊((2B + 31) × L) / 100⌋ + L + 10 + SPHP

Attack/Sp.Attack/Defense/Sp.Defense/Speed: Stat = ⌊((2B + 31) × L / 100⌋ + 5 + SP) × N⌋

Where: B = base stat, L = level, IV = 31 (fixed), SP = stat points, N = nature multiplier
